About AURA

The first workshop on agents that do research.

Large language model agents are beginning to carry out the full arc of scientific research — generating hypotheses, designing and running experiments, analyzing results, and communicating findings.

AURA convenes researchers across machine learning, agents, evaluation, and the science of science to chart a path toward autonomous research systems that are both capable and trustworthy. We welcome work spanning agent architectures, automated experimentation, evaluation and reproducibility, safety and oversight, and real-world scientific discovery.
